The legend of the witches' road

The legend says the road was created by a circle of twelve witches who gathered under a blood moon centuries ago. They sought to connect the physical world with the mystical, weaving spells into the very stones and trees that line the path. Their goal was not conquest or destruction but the preservation of magical knowledge and harmony between realms.
